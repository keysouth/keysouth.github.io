About Key South

Key South is a music composer specializing in sync placements for TV, film, commercials, and trailers. Inspired by some of the greats, his compositions evoke emotion and create vivid atmosphere for visual storytelling — and most importantly, sound amazing.

Key's unique blend of hip hop, orchestral, and electronic elements gives his music a distinctive, modern edge — spanning atmospheric and cinematic to orchestral trap, dramedy, and beyond.
